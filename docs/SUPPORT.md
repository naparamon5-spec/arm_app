# Support — Ardent Resource Management (ARM)

**Ardent Resource Management (ARM)** is an internal enterprise app for authorized Ardent Networks employees, managers, and approvers to review and approve quotes.

## Getting Started

1. Open the ARM app.
2. Sign in with your **Ardent company email address** (e.g., `you@ardentnetworks.com.ph`) and your password.
3. Enable **Remember Me** to stay signed in on your device.

## Common Questions

**I can't sign in.**
Make sure you are using your Ardent company email and correct password. Accounts are provisioned by Ardent IT — if your credentials don't work, contact your administrator.

**I forgot my password.**
Passwords are managed by Ardent Networks IT. Contact your IT administrator to reset it.

**How do I get an account?**
Accounts are created and managed by Ardent Networks IT for authorized employees. They cannot be created within the app. Contact your administrator to request access.

**How do I review or approve a quote?**
Open a quote from the dashboard or approvals list to view its full details, line items, and supporting documents, then take the available action.

**How do I view a quote's attachments?**
Open the quote and select the attached PDF or document to view it in the app.

**I'm having a technical problem or found a bug.**
Please contact support using the details below and include your device model, iOS version, and a description of the issue.
