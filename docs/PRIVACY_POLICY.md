# Privacy Policy — Ardent Resource Management (ARM)

**Last updated:** 24 July 2026

Ardent Networks Inc. ("Ardent," "we," "us," or "our") operates the **Ardent Resource Management (ARM)** mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

ARM is an internal enterprise application intended solely for authorized Ardent Networks employees, managers, and approvers. It is not offered to the general public.

## 1. Information We Collect

The App collects the minimum information required to authenticate you and provide its functionality:

- **Corporate email address** — You enter your Ardent company email address to sign in. It is transmitted to our servers to authenticate your identity and is linked to your user account.
- **Password** — Used only to authenticate your sign-in request. It is transmitted securely and is not stored on the device in readable form.

We do **not** collect your location, contacts, photos, advertising identifiers, or any device analytics. The App contains **no** third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking software.

## 2. Information Displayed in the App

After you sign in, the App retrieves and displays business information from Ardent's systems, including your profile details (name, role, employee ID, classification) and quote records (customers, pricing, and supporting documents) that you are authorized to review and approve. This information originates from Ardent's servers for display within the App and is governed by Ardent's internal data policies.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use the information collected to:

- Authenticate your identity and maintain your signed-in session;
- Provide access to quotes and documents you are authorized to review;
- Enable you to approve or act on quotes and manage your profile;
- Maintain the security and integrity of the service.

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- All communication between the App and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS/TLS.
- Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using the operating system's secure storage (Keychain on iOS) and are used to keep you signed in when "Remember Me" is enabled.
- Access to business data is restricted to authorized Ardent personnel.

## 5. Data Retention

Authentication and session data are retained only as long as necessary to provide the service. Business records are retained in accordance with Ardent Networks' internal data-retention policies.

## 6. Accounts

User accounts are provisioned and managed by Ardent Networks IT for authorized employees. Accounts cannot be created or deleted within the App. To request account changes or removal, contact your Ardent IT administrator.

## 7. Tracking

The App does **not** track you across apps or websites owned by other companies and does not use the device advertising identifier (IDFA).

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by Ardent Networks employees and is not directed to children under 13.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
